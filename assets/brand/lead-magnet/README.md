# BAZ 90-Day Growth Playbook

Lead magnet. Give this away in exchange for an email.

## Files

- `playbook-cover.png` — 8.5×11" cover (PNG, 1200×1600, 219KB). Use as the first page of the PDF, or as a standalone image in a landing page.
- `playbook-body.md` — 24-page playbook content (markdown). Convert to PDF with pandoc: `pandoc playbook-body.md -o playbook.pdf`

## The ask

A founder downloads this in exchange for an email. The email goes into the BAZ nurture sequence. Within 7 days, they get a case study. Within 14, a "still thinking about it?" email. Within 21, an invite to book a 30-minute call.

The playbook itself is the bait. Make it good. Make it real. Make it specific. Don't make it generic marketing advice.

## Conversion path

```
Landing page (/insights/playbook or /playbook)
  ↓ fill email
PDF download (this asset)
  ↓
Email #1: welcome + 1 case study
Email #3: 1 more case study
Email #7: "still thinking about it?" + calendar link
```

## Don't

- Don't gate the playbook behind a phone number. Email only.
- Don't put "BAZ" branding on every page. The playbook should be valuable on its own — BAZ gets credit at the end, not throughout.
- Don't pad to 24 pages. If it's 16 pages of real content, ship 16.
