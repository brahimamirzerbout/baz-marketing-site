# Split Forgot Password Card With Illustration

Create a forgot-password page split into two columns on large 1280px width breakpoints inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding.

**Left column** contains a single elevated card with rounded corners and a light surface treatment, vertically centered within the column height. Inside the card, stack from top to bottom: a small brand icon or logo mark on its own line paired with a brand name label immediately to the right of the icon on the same baseline; a bold heading on its own line directly below the brand row (e.g. "Forgot your password?"); a supporting paragraph of one or two sentences directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width, reassuring the user and explaining the reset flow; a labeled email input row placed below the paragraph (the label sits above the input on its own line with a tiny required-field marker to the right of the label text; the input carries a leading envelope icon affordance flush-left inside the field and a placeholder suggesting an email address); a single checkbox row containing a small square checkbox at the left and an inline label whose text confirms acceptance of linked terms and conditions (with the terms phrase rendered as an inline text link); one full-width primary action button at the bottom of the card with a short label (e.g. "Reset password").

**Right column** holds one large illustrative scene that fills most of the column area, vertically centered within the column. The illustration depicts a figure sitting at a workstation with a monitor displaying a user profile or authentication interface, plants or office elements in the background, conveying a calm account-recovery theme without relying on photographic imagery.

On narrow viewports stack the two columns vertically with the form card first and the illustration directly below it; keep all internal stacks in their original reading order with comfortable side padding.
