# Split Forgot Password With Technology Showcase

Create a forgot-password page split into two columns on large 1280px width breakpoints inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding.

**Left column** is a left-aligned vertical stack, vertically centered within the column height, containing a compact reset-password form. Stack from top to bottom: a bold heading on its own line (e.g. "Forgot your password?"); a supporting paragraph of one or two sentences directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width, reassuring the user and explaining the reset flow; a labeled email input row placed below the paragraph after extra vertical space (the label sits above the input on its own line with a tiny required-field marker to the right of the label text; the input carries a leading envelope icon affordance flush-left inside the field and a placeholder suggesting an email address); a single checkbox row containing a small square checkbox at the left and an inline label whose text confirms acceptance of linked terms and conditions (with the terms phrase rendered as an inline text link); one full-width primary action button below the checkbox with a short label (e.g. "Reset password").

**Right column** uses a subtle filled background with rounded corners and contains a centered vertical stack vertically centered within the column height. Inside the column, place a circular arrangement of technology or partner brand icons distributed evenly around an invisible orbital ring (each icon sits inside a small rounded container); below the orbital graphic, place a bold centered heading stating a technology count or integration metric on its own line (e.g. "Over 200+ technologies"); a centered supporting paragraph of one or two sentences directly below the heading describing the platform's integration breadth.

On narrow viewports stack the two columns vertically with the form column first and the technology showcase directly below it; keep all internal stacks in their original reading order with comfortable side padding.
