# Announcement Application Navbar

Create an application navbar composed of two fixed horizontal bars stacked at the top of the viewport, both spanning the full viewport width edge to edge with no surrounding page padding and no enclosing max-width container around the bars themselves.

**Announcement banner** is pinned to the very top of the viewport as a slim full-width horizontal strip with a subtly different background to separate it from the navbar below. Inside the strip, center a single inline message group on the same baseline composed of a short multi-word marketing line followed by a trailing inline link composed of a short labeled call-to-action (such as "Join PRO") with a small trailing arrow icon.

**Primary navbar** sits directly below the announcement banner as a full-width horizontal bar divided into three regions on the same baseline. On the left side of the bar, place a hamburger menu icon followed by a brand mark composed of a small logo icon paired with the brand name to its right on the same baseline. In the center of the bar, place a horizontal row of navigation items sitting side by side with consistent spacing between them; each navigation item is a small vertical stack containing a small icon centered on the top line and a short text label centered directly beneath the icon (rather than a traditional inline icon-and-label row). On the right end of the bar, group a small icon-only theme toggle, a small icon-only notification bell, a small circular user avatar, and a text logout link sitting side by side on the same baseline.

**Sizing rules.** The navbar spans the full viewport width edge to edge with no surrounding page padding and no enclosing max-width container around the bars themselves. Do not generate any main content area or placeholder blocks below the navbar; only output the navbar code. Every visible action button inside the navbar (primary, secondary outlined, dashed, and any inline composite action button with a label) shares one sizing rule: 12 pixels of horizontal padding on each side, 6 pixels of vertical padding on top and bottom, label text at 12 pixels in a medium font weight, and any small leading or trailing icon aligned to the label's vertical center. This button sizing rule does not apply to icon-only utility buttons, to text-only navigation links, or to user-identity buttons. Colors, borders, surfaces, and hover or focus states stay agnostic and follow the host design system.

On narrow viewports the announcement banner may truncate its message or hide behind a dismiss toggle; the centered icon-label navigation collapses behind the hamburger menu icon; the right utility group reduces to essential elements (avatar and overflow menu). Preserve reading order throughout.
