# Centered 404 Not Found Page

Create a 404 page as one centered vertical stack on a full-width section background, vertically centered within the viewport height. Stack from top to bottom: a small brand icon or logo mark centered horizontally on its own line; a brand name label centered on its own line directly below the icon; a large bold centered heading reading the error status and a short descriptor (e.g. "404 Not Found") on its own line directly below the brand name; a single centered paragraph of one short sentence explaining that the page does not exist, placed directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width.

On narrow viewports keep the same single-column centered stack with comfortable side padding; allow the heading to wrap naturally while preserving center alignment throughout.
