# Newsletter Sidebar Blog Feed

Create a blog section split into two columns on large breakpoints inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding.

**Left column** (narrower, roughly one-third width) is a left-aligned vertical stack containing, in order: a large left-aligned heading on its own line (e.g. "Our Blog"); a supporting paragraph directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width; a thin horizontal divider spanning the full column width; a newsletter signup block below the divider composed of a bold sub-heading on its own line (e.g. "Sign up for our newsletter"), an email input field with a placeholder label and a small leading mail icon on its own line directly below the sub-heading, a compact primary "Subscribe" button sitting immediately to the right of the input on the same baseline, and a short line of fine-print copy directly below the input referencing a privacy policy text link.

**Right column** (wider, roughly two-thirds width) is a vertical stack of article cards separated by consistent vertical spacing. Each card is a horizontal-feeling panel containing, in order from top to bottom: a top row composed of a small pill-shaped category badge with a tiny leading icon and short label on the left and a small time-ago indicator with a tiny clock icon and short label anchored to the right of the same baseline; a bold multi-line headline rendered as a clickable link on its own line directly below the top row; a short excerpt paragraph directly below the headline; a bottom row composed of a small circular avatar on the left, the author name and a publication date with read-time label stacked in two lines to the right of the avatar, and a text-style "Read more" link with a trailing arrow icon anchored to the far right of the same baseline.

On narrow viewports stack the two columns vertically with the sidebar column first and the article cards directly below it; keep all internal stacks in their original reading order.
