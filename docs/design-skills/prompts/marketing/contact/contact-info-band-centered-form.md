# Contact Info Band With Centered Form

Create a contact section in two vertical stages inside a centered page container constrained to a 1280px width with comfortable side padding.

**Top stage** is a three-column row on large breakpoints with equal column widths and comfortable column gaps. Each cell is a left-aligned vertical stack opening with a small icon at the top (keyed to the cell theme), followed by a bold short title on its own line, then body lines beneath the title: the first cell titles address with city or region name, street line, and postal code each on their own lines; the second cell titles company information with legal entity name and tax identifier each on their own lines; the third cell titles contact info with a short sentence about when to email on its own line and a single general mailto address on the next line. On narrow viewports stack the three cells vertically in reading order with generous spacing between cells.

**Bottom stage** is a form constrained to 640px width and centered horizontally beneath the info band. The form arranges personal fields in a two-column grid on wide layouts with comfortable column gaps: required first-name input on the left (label above with required-field marker; short example placeholder inside) and last-name input on the right (same shape); required email input on the left (label above with required-field marker; company-style placeholder inside) and phone-number composite on the right (a narrow country-or-region prefix select control beside a wider numeric input with spaced-digit placeholder, both on the same baseline); a country single-select on the left (label above; defaulting sensibly) and a language single-select on the right (same shape). Beneath the two-column grid, span a full-width labeled multi-line message textarea (the label sits above on its own line with a required-field marker and an optional small circular question-mark information affordance to the right; the textarea carries a placeholder and allows vertical expansion). Finish with a single checkbox row containing a small square checkbox at the left and an inline label whose text links to the terms of service, then one full-width primary submit button beneath the checkbox whose label combines short send text and a trailing send-arrow glyph.

On narrow viewports collapse the two-column field grid into a single-column stack, keeping the same top-to-bottom reading order.
