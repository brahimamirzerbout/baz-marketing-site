# Centered Contact Form With Footer Band

Create a contact section as a single centered vertical stack inside a centered page container constrained to a 1280px width with comfortable side padding.

At the top, place an intro block centered horizontally: a main heading on its own line, and a welcoming paragraph about inquiries, feedback, and partnerships on its own line directly below the heading.

Below the intro block, center a card-like container with rounded corners and a moderate max width. Inside the card, arrange a form in a two-column field grid on wide layouts with comfortable column gaps: required first-name input on the left (label above with required-field marker; short appropriate placeholder inside — avoid reusing an email-style placeholder on name fields) and required last-name input on the right (same shape); required email input spanning full width on its own row (label above with required-field marker; appropriate email placeholder inside); required phone row composed of a narrow country-prefix select control beside a wider numeric input with spaced-digit placeholder, both on the same baseline spanning full width. Beneath the grid, span a full-width labeled multi-line message textarea (the label sits above on its own line with a required-field marker and an optional small circular question-mark information affordance to the right; the textarea carries a placeholder and allows vertical expansion). Below the textarea, place a single checkbox row containing a small square checkbox at the left and an inline label whose text confirms agreement to the terms of service (with the terms-of-service phrase rendered as an inline text link). Finish the card with one full-width primary submit button whose label combines short send text and a trailing send-or-arrow glyph.

Below the card, add a three-column footer band on large breakpoints with equal column widths and comfortable column gaps. Each column is a centered vertical stack containing, in order: a small icon at the top (keyed to the column theme); a short column heading ending with a colon on its own line beneath the icon; a short descriptive sentence on its own line beneath the heading; and a concrete action element on its own line beneath the sentence. The first column is themed around email and carries a mailto text link as its action; the second column is themed around phone and carries a formatted telephone number as its action; the third column is themed around support and carries either a text link or a small button navigating to a support center as its action.

On narrow viewports, collapse the form grid into a single-column stack inside the card, and stack the three footer columns vertically in reading order with generous spacing between them.
