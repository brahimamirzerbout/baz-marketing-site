# Full-Width Contact Directory Section

Create a contact section that reads as a single centered vertical stack constrained to a 1280px width container with comfortable side padding.

At the top, place a large multi-line heading (sentence case or title case) on its own line, left-aligned within the container. Directly below the heading, place one short supporting line about guidance, support, and tailored solutions on its own line.

Below the supporting line, span a single full-width horizontal media band (photograph or illustration) that stretches across the entire content width, with a moderate aspect ratio so it reads as a cinematic strip.

Under the media band, stack three horizontal directory bands separated by generous vertical rhythm (each band separated from the next by whitespace or a subtle horizontal rule).

**First band** begins with a small section label prefixed by a decorative turn or bullet-like marker, left-aligned on its own line. Below the label, the band splits into two equal columns on large 1280px width breakpoints: the left column carries a short category label (general hello) on its own line followed by a single email address on the next line; the right column carries a short category label (enquiries) on its own line followed by a different email address on the next line.

**Second band** follows the same structure: a small prefixed section label on its own line, then two columns below. The left column carries a postal-address label on its own line and a full multi-line street address beneath it; the right column carries a map-related label on its own line and a text link to get directions beneath it.

**Third band** follows the same structure: a small prefixed section label on its own line, then two columns below. The left column carries a social-platforms label on its own line and a vertical list of platform names rendered as text links beneath it; the right column carries a phone label on its own line and one or more listed telephone numbers beneath it.

On narrow viewports stack the two columns within each band vertically (left column first, right column second) while keeping the section labels and media band at full width.
