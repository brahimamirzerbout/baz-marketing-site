# Split Card App CTA

Create a CTA section framed as one wide horizontal card with a thin outer border and softly rounded corners, sitting inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding. Inside the card, split into two halves on large 1280 px width breakpoints.

The **left half** is a left-aligned vertical stack containing, in order from top to bottom: a large multi-line heading on its own line; a supporting paragraph at a comfortable reading width directly beneath the heading; a horizontal row of two store-download buttons sitting side by side **on one line beside each other** (each button carries a small leading platform glyph at the left followed by standard store wording to the right of the glyph — the two buttons share the same height and the row never wraps to a second line; treat this as the global two-button row rule). Give the left half generous internal padding so the copy and buttons breathe within the card surface.

The **right half** fills entirely with a decorative field of light diagonal or diamond-line grid texture. The pattern carries no essential text and runs edge to edge within the right half of the card so it reads as a purely ornamental surface. Allow the decorative field to share the card's rounded corner radius on its outward-facing corners.

On narrow viewports, stack the two halves vertically with the copy-and-buttons half first and the patterned decorative field beneath it; let the decorative field span the full card width at a reduced height.
