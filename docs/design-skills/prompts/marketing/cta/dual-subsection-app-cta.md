# Dual Subsection App CTA

Create a CTA section as two large vertical subsections separated by generous vertical space, all inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding.

**First subsection:** split into two columns on large 1280 px width breakpoints. The left column is a left-aligned vertical stack containing, in order from top to bottom: a horizontal row of two small square platform icons sitting side by side on one line; a large heading on its own line beneath the icon row; a body paragraph at a comfortable reading width directly beneath the heading; a vertical list of approximately three items where each item begins with a small circular check icon at the left and a single descriptive line to the right of the icon on the same baseline; a short closing sentence on its own line beneath the list; a text link whose label includes a small trailing rightward arrow at the end of the link text (the link sits on its own line beneath the closing sentence). The right column centers one phone mockup whose screen shows a dashboard-style layout with a chart region and a map region visible inside the device frame. Vertically center the mockup relative to the text column.

**Second subsection:** reverse the column order so the phone mockup sits on the left and the text column sits on the right. The left column centers one phone mockup whose screen shows several stacked floating metric cards with softly rounded corners and light elevation (the cards appear to float above a background surface inside the device frame). The right column is a left-aligned vertical stack containing, in order from top to bottom: a large heading on its own line; a first body paragraph directly beneath the heading; a second body paragraph directly beneath the first; a thin horizontal divider spanning the column width; a vertical list of approximately five items where each item begins with a small circular check icon at the left and a single descriptive line to the right of the icon on the same baseline; a horizontal row containing a solid primary button (with a small trailing rightward arrow at the right of its label) and a bordered secondary button placed to the right of the primary button **beside each other on one row** (the row never wraps to a second line — treat this as the global two-button row rule).

On narrow viewports, stack each subsection's columns so copy appears before the phone mockup in both blocks (text column first, mockup second) for consistent reading order; state this choice in implementation.
