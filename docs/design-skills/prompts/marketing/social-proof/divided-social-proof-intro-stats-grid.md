# Divided Social Proof Intro With Stats Grid

Create a social proof section split into two columns on large 1280px width breakpoints inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding. Place a thin vertical divider between the two columns running the full height of the column area.

The **left column** is a left-aligned vertical stack containing, in order: a small pill-shaped badge on its own line (composed of a short category label inside a rounded container); a large left-aligned multi-line heading on its own line directly below the badge; a supporting paragraph directly beneath the heading at a comfortable reading width; two text-style links stacked vertically, each on its own line, whose labels include a trailing arrow icon.

The **right column** contains a two-column by two-row grid of four stat blocks with consistent gutters between them. Each stat block is a centered vertical stack composed of, in order: a small icon centered at the top; one large prominent numeric value on its own line directly below the icon; a short descriptive label directly beneath the value.

On narrow viewports stack the two columns vertically with the intro column first and the stat grid directly beneath it; remove the vertical divider and preserve all internal stacks in their original reading order.
