# Social Proof Bento Showcase

Create a social proof section inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding. At the top, place a centered header block composed of a large heading centered on its own line followed by a centered supporting paragraph directly beneath it at a moderate reading width. Below the paragraph, place a horizontal row of two text links sitting side by side on the same baseline, centered as a pair; each link label includes a trailing arrow icon.

Below the header, place a bento-style grid composed of four stat cards arranged around a tall central showcase panel. On large 1280px width breakpoints, the layout reads as a three-column structure: the left column stacks two stat cards vertically, the center column holds the tall showcase panel spanning the full height of both rows, and the right column stacks two stat cards vertically.

Each stat card is a rounded rectangular panel containing a left-aligned vertical stack composed of, in order: a small rounded-square icon container at the top (holding a single centered glyph); a bold stat headline on its own line directly below the icon (combining a prominent numeric value with a short label); a short multi-line description paragraph directly beneath the headline; and a compact outlined button at the bottom of the card whose label is composed of a small leading icon and a short action label on the same baseline.

The central showcase panel is a tall rounded rectangular region containing a centered vertical stack composed of a loose cluster of small technology or brand logo icons arranged in an organic scattered composition in the upper portion, followed by a large bold headline on its own line centered below the logo cluster and a short centered description paragraph directly beneath the headline.

On narrow viewports collapse the bento grid into a single column stacking the top-left card, the central panel, the top-right card, the bottom-left card, and the bottom-right card in reading order; each card and the showcase panel stretch to the full content width with comfortable side padding.
