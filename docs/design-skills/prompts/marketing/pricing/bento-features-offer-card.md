# Bento Features with Offer Card

Create a pricing section split into two columns on large 1280 px width breakpoints inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding.

**Left column** is a vertically stacked block containing, in order: a two-column grid of small rounded "bento" cells of uniform size (e.g. six cells arranged in three rows of two) — inside each cell, stack from top to bottom: a small icon sitting inside a rounded square container (subtle border, simple line icon), a bold feature title on its own line directly below the icon container, and a short supporting paragraph directly beneath the title at a comfortable reading width. Directly below the bento grid, after extra vertical space, place a trust band: a small label on its own line (e.g. "Trusted by" copy) followed by a horizontal row of brand logos, each rendered as a small monochrome glyph with consistent spacing between them.

**Right column** is a single tall offer card with rounded corners whose height roughly matches the combined height of the bento grid and trust band. The card interior is divided into horizontal bands by thin separators, stacked from top to bottom:

**Header band** — a small icon container anchored at the top left of the band; a small pill or ribbon label anchored at the top right (e.g. billing type); a large plan title on its own line below the icon and label row; a single-line tagline directly below the title; a dominant price with a contextual suffix (e.g. "per seat", "per project", or "per month") on the same baseline to the right of the amount; and a line of fine-print copy directly below the price.

**Selection band** — a short label on its own line followed by a horizontal segmented control offering a few mutually exclusive options (e.g. tool or platform names) with one segment visually selected at a time.

**Feature band** — a vertical list of feature rows where each row is composed of a small circular check icon at the left, a bold feature title to its right on the same baseline, and a smaller description line directly beneath the title, indented to align with the title text rather than the icon.

**Footer band** — one full-width primary call-to-action button; directly below it, a social-proof row combining a tight horizontal cluster of overlapping small circular avatar thumbnails on the left and a short caption line to their right.

Centered directly below the offer card on its own line (still within the right column or spanning the full section width), place a single reassurance line composed of a small leading check icon and short guarantee copy on the same baseline.

On narrow viewports, stack the two columns vertically in reading order — the bento grid and trust band first, then the offer card and guarantee line; each internal stack preserves its original reading order.
