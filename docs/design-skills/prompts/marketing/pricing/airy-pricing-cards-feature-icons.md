# Airy Pricing Cards with Feature Icons

Create a pricing section as one vertically stacked block inside a max-width container. At the top, place a centered heading on its own line; directly below the heading, a centered supporting paragraph at a comfortable reading width.

Below the heading block, place a horizontal row of three pricing cards of equal width with consistent gutters between them. Each card uses a transparent background with no visible border so it reads as an open, airy surface. Inside each card, stack from top to bottom: a plan name on its own line; a short single-line description directly below the name; a large dominant price with a small "/month" suffix on the same baseline to the right of the amount; a vertical list of feature rows — each row composed of a unique small illustrative icon anchored at the left (distinct per feature, not a uniform check mark), a short bold feature title to the right of the icon on the same baseline, and a short helper line of smaller copy directly beneath the title, indented to align with the title text rather than the icon; and one full-width primary call-to-action button placed at the bottom of the card.

On narrow viewports the three cards stack vertically in their original left-to-right order; each card stretches to the full content width with comfortable side padding.
