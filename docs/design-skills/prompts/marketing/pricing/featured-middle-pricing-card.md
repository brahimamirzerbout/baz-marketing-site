# Featured Middle Pricing Card

Create a pricing section as one vertically stacked block inside a max-width container. At the top, place a centered heading on its own line; directly below the heading, a centered supporting paragraph at a comfortable reading width.

Below the heading block, place a horizontal row of three pricing cards of equal width. The middle card is visually elevated (taller than the two side cards, sitting on a distinct filled background) and carries a small "Most popular" pill badge anchored to the center of its top edge. Inside each card, stack from top to bottom: a plan name on its own line; a short single-line description directly below the name; a large dominant price with a small "/month" suffix on the same baseline to the right of the amount; one full-width primary call-to-action button (the middle card's button may use a contrasting treatment to reinforce its featured status); and a vertical list of feature rows (each row composed of a small circular check icon at the left and a short feature label to its right on the same baseline).

On narrow viewports the three cards stack vertically with the highlighted middle card preserving its position in the sequence; each card stretches to the full content width with comfortable side padding.
