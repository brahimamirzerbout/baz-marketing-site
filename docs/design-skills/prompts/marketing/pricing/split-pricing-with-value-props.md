# Split Pricing with Value Props

Create a pricing section split into two columns on large 1280 px width breakpoints inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding.

**Left column** is a left-aligned vertical stack containing, in order: a small label badge (pill shape) on its own line; a large left-aligned heading on a line directly below the badge; a supporting paragraph directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width; a vertical list of high-level value propositions (each row composed of a small circular check icon at the left and a short label to its right on the same baseline); and a horizontal row of three brand logos placed below the value list after extra vertical space, each logo rendered as a small monochrome glyph with consistent spacing between them.

**Right column** contains a single transparent pricing card (no visible background fill, no outer border) with generous padding. Inside the card, stack from top to bottom: a plan name on its own line; a large dominant price with a small "/month" suffix on the same baseline to the right of the amount; a thin horizontal divider spanning the full card width; a two-column grid of eight to ten feature labels (each cell composed of a small circular check icon at the left and a short label to its right on the same baseline) covering the benefits for this plan; and one full-width primary call-to-action button at the bottom of the card (e.g. "Get started").

On narrow viewports the two columns stack vertically with the content column first and the pricing card second; each internal stack preserves its original reading order.
