# Three Pricing Cards

Create a pricing section as one vertically stacked block on a full-width background inside a max-width container. At the top, place a centered section heading on its own line; directly below the heading, a centered supporting paragraph at a comfortable reading width.

Below the heading block, place a horizontal row of three pricing cards of equal width with consistent gutters between them. Inside each card, stack from top to bottom: a plan name on its own line; a single-line plan description directly below the name; a large dominant price paired with a small "/month" suffix sitting on the same baseline to the right of the amount; a thin horizontal divider spanning the full card width; a vertical list of feature rows (each row composed of a small circular check icon anchored at the left and a short feature label to its right on the same baseline); and one full-width primary call-to-action button whose label text is centered within the button, placed at the bottom of the card.

On narrow viewports the three cards stack vertically in their original left-to-right reading order; each card stretches to the full content width with comfortable side padding.
