# Split Pricing Card with Feature Grid

Create a pricing section as one vertically stacked block on a full-width background inside a max-width container. At the top, place a full-width left-aligned heading on its own line; directly below the heading, a left-aligned supporting paragraph at a comfortable reading width.

Below the heading block, place one full-width bordered card whose interior is split into two columns on large 1280 px width breakpoints with a thin vertical divider running between them.

**Left column** of the card is a vertically centered, padded vertical stack containing, in order: a plan name on its own line; a short single-line description directly below the name; a very large dominant price paired with a small "/month" suffix sitting on the same baseline to the right of the amount; and one full-width primary call-to-action button whose label text is centered within the button.

**Right column** of the card contains a two-column grid of short feature labels (roughly five rows by two columns of items); each cell is composed of a small circular check icon anchored at the left and a short feature label to its right on the same baseline.

Below the card, centered directly beneath it on its own line, place a single small helper-text line (e.g. a money-back guarantee notice) at a muted weight.

On narrow viewports the card's two columns stack vertically with the plan-and-price column first and the feature grid second; the helper line remains centered below.
