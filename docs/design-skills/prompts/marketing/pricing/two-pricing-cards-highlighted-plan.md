# Two Pricing Cards with Highlighted Plan

Create a pricing section as one vertically stacked block inside a max-width container. At the top, place a centered heading on its own line; directly below the heading, a centered supporting paragraph at a comfortable reading width.

Below the heading block, place exactly two pricing cards side by side, each taking half of the container width with a consistent gutter between them. The right card is the highlighted plan and carries a small "Recommended" pill badge anchored to the center of its top edge. Inside each card, stack from top to bottom: a plan name on its own line; a short single-line description directly below the name; a large dominant price with a small "/month" suffix on the same baseline to the right of the amount; one full-width primary call-to-action button (the highlighted card's button may use a contrasting treatment); a thin horizontal divider spanning the full card width; and a vertical list of feature rows (each row composed of a small circular check icon at the left and a short feature label to its right on the same baseline).

On narrow viewports the two cards stack vertically in their original left-to-right order; each card stretches to the full content width with comfortable side padding.
