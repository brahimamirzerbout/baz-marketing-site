# Pricing Table with Billing Toggle

Create a pricing section as one vertically stacked block inside a max-width container. At the top, place a full-width left-aligned heading on its own line; directly below the heading, a left-aligned supporting paragraph at a comfortable reading width.

Directly below the paragraph, place a horizontal two-option toggle control (one segment labeled "Monthly", the other labeled "Yearly") with one segment visually selected at a time. Anchored to the right of the "Yearly" segment on the same baseline, place a small pill badge displaying a savings percentage (e.g. "Save X%").

Below the toggle, place one full-width bordered card surface whose interior is divided into three equal-width plan columns separated by thin vertical dividers (the three plans live inside a single shared card, not as three separate cards). Inside each column, stack from top to bottom: a plan name on its own line; a short single-line description directly below the name; a large dominant price with a billing-cadence suffix (e.g. "/month" or "/year") on the same baseline to the right of the amount; a secondary line of smaller copy directly below the price showing the alternate-cadence price; one full-width primary call-to-action button; and a vertical list of feature rows (each row composed of a small circular check icon at the left and a short feature label to its right on the same baseline).

On narrow viewports the toggle stays centered on its own line, and the three plan columns break out of the shared card and stack vertically as individual cards in their original left-to-right reading order; each card stretches to the full content width with comfortable side padding.
