# Centered Hero with Image Gallery

Create a hero as one centered vertical stack on a full-width section background. Stack from top to bottom: a large centered heading on its own line; a centered supporting paragraph directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width; a horizontal row of two CTA buttons sitting side by side on the same baseline (a primary button on the left and a secondary button on the right), centered as a pair beneath the paragraph; finally, an asymmetric image collage placed directly below the button row inside a single bounding row. The collage is composed of one large image on the left occupying roughly two-thirds of the row width and two smaller images stacked vertically on the right occupying the remaining width (the two right images share the same total height as the single left image, separated by a small vertical gutter). On narrow viewports keep the heading, paragraph, and buttons in a single-column centered stack, and collapse the collage into a single vertical column of images in reading order (large image first, then the two smaller images beneath it).
