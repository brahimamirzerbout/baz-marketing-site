# Editorial Hero with Featured Post and Article List

Create a hero split into two columns on large 1280px width breakpoints inside a centered page container with generous spacing between the columns.

**Left column** is one featured article as a vertical stack containing, in order: a large wide image with rounded corners at the top spanning the full column width; a small category pill composed of a tiny leading icon and a short label on its own line directly below the image; a prominent left-aligned headline on its own line directly below the pill; a horizontal author row on the same baseline composed of a small circular avatar on the left, the author name immediately to the right of the avatar, and a publication date label to the right of the name; a short excerpt paragraph directly below the author row at a comfortable reading width; a text-style "read more" link composed of a short label paired with a trailing arrow icon on its own line at the bottom.

**Right column** is a vertical stack of three compact article blocks with equal structure. Each block is a vertical stack composed of a small category pill (a tiny leading icon paired with a short label) on its own line at the top; a medium headline on its own line directly below the pill; a two- or three-line excerpt paragraph directly below the headline; and the same text-style "read more" link with a trailing arrow icon at the bottom of the block.

On narrow viewports stack the two columns vertically with the featured column first and the list of three articles directly beneath it; keep all internal stacks in their original reading order.
