# Centered Hero with Video Thumbnail

Create a hero as one centered vertical stack on a full-width section background. Stack from top to bottom: a large centered heading on its own line; a centered supporting paragraph directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width; a horizontal row of two CTA buttons sitting side by side on the same baseline (a primary button on the left and a secondary button on the right), centered as a pair beneath the paragraph; finally, a large centered video thumbnail (a static image with rounded corners) placed directly below the button row that spans most of the container width, with a circular play-button overlay centered on top of the thumbnail (the play icon sits at the exact visual center of the image). On narrow viewports keep the same single-column centered stack, let the two CTA buttons wrap into a vertical pair if needed, and allow the thumbnail to span the full content width with comfortable side padding.
