# Split Hero Section with Product Screenshot and Floating Cards

Create a hero split into two columns on large 1280px width breakpoints inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding.

**Left column** is a left-aligned vertical stack containing, in order: a small pill-shaped badge composed of a tiny leading icon and a short label on its own line; a left-aligned heading on its own line directly below the badge; a supporting paragraph directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width; a horizontal row of two CTA buttons sitting side by side on the same baseline (a primary button on the left and a secondary button on the right), placed beneath the paragraph.

**Right column** holds one large product screenshot that fills the column area, with two small floating UI cards overlaid on top of the screenshot (one card anchored near the top-left edge of the image and one card anchored near the bottom-right edge). Each floating card is a compact rounded container with a light elevated treatment showing a short metric or status detail.

On narrow viewports stack the two columns vertically with the content column first and the screenshot directly below it; the floating cards maintain their relative positions on the image.
