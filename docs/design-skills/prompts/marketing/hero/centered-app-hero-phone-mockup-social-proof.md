# Centered App Hero with Phone Mockup and Social Proof

Create a hero as one centered vertical stack on a full-width section background that includes a very subtle repeating geometric grid or dot texture behind the content.

**Copy region** contains a centered heading on its own line with a centered supporting paragraph directly beneath it at a comfortable reading width.

**Badge row** sits directly below the paragraph: a centered horizontal pair of app-distribution buttons styled like official store badges on the same baseline (each badge is a compact rounded container composed of a small platform mark on the left and two stacked lines of label text to the right of the mark).

**Phone mockup** sits directly below the badge row: one large centered portrait phone mockup whose screen shows an in-app dashboard. Inside the phone screen, stack from top to bottom: a summary chart region; a short stats section title on its own line; several stacked metric rows (each row is composed of a small sparkline-style chart on the left, a primary numeric value in the middle, and a change indicator on the right, all on the same baseline); a full-width primary action button; a bottom tab bar with a few icon slots.

**Social proof row** sits directly below the phone mockup: a centered single horizontal row composed of slightly overlapping circular user avatars on the left, a compact row of star icons immediately to the right of the avatars on the same baseline, and a short line of text to the right of the stars stating a numeric rating and a review count.

On narrow viewports keep the column centered, allow the badge row and social-proof row to wrap into stacked arrangements while preserving reading order, and let the phone mockup scale to fit the content width with comfortable side padding.
