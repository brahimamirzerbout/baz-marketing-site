# Centered Hero with Dashboard Mockup

Create a hero as one centered vertical stack on a full-width section background. Stack from top to bottom: a small horizontal announcement pill composed of a tiny tag label on the left, a short sentence in the middle, and a trailing chevron icon on the right (the entire pill is one link element on its own line); a large centered heading on its own line directly below the pill; a centered supporting paragraph directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width; a horizontal row of two CTA buttons sitting side by side on the same baseline (a primary button on the left and a secondary button on the right), centered as a pair beneath the paragraph; finally, a single large browser or dashboard mockup image, centered horizontally and clipped at the bottom edge of the section so it appears to extend below the fold (the mockup spans most of the container width). On narrow viewports keep the same single-column centered stack, let the two CTA buttons wrap into a vertical pair if needed, and allow the mockup image to span the full content width with comfortable side padding.
