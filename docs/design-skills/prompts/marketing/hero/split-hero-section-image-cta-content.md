# Split Hero Section with Image and CTA Content

Create a hero split into two equal columns on large 1280px width breakpoints inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding.

**Left column** is filled entirely by one large image that spans the full width and height of the column (the image covers the column area edge-to-edge with no internal padding).

**Right column** is a left-aligned vertical stack containing, in order: a small pill-shaped badge composed of a tiny leading icon and a short label on its own line; a left-aligned heading on its own line directly below the badge; a supporting paragraph directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width; a horizontal row of two CTA buttons sitting side by side on the same baseline (a primary button on the left and a secondary button on the right), placed at the bottom of the column beneath the paragraph.

On narrow viewports stack the two columns vertically with the image column first and the content column directly below it; keep all internal stacks in their original reading order.
