# Hero Section with Stats and Converter Panel

Create a hero as one centered vertical stack on a full-width section background.

**Top region** contains a centered heading on its own line with a centered supporting paragraph directly beneath it at a comfortable reading width.

**Stat row** sits directly below the paragraph: a single horizontal row of five equal-width stat cards on the same baseline. Each card is a compact outlined container composed of one prominent numeric or short value on its own line at the top and a short descriptive label directly beneath the value.

**Converter panel** sits directly below the stat row: one large full-width centered panel with rounded corners styled as an interactive product mockup. Inside the panel, stack from top to bottom: a two-option segmented control (or tab pair) centered horizontally on its own line; two side-by-side field groups on the same baseline in the middle of the panel, where each group is a vertical stack composed of a short label on its own line, a dropdown-style control for choosing an option directly below the label, and a numeric input directly below the dropdown; one full-width primary CTA button at the bottom of the panel. Optionally treat the panel interior with a subtle geometric grid texture so it reads as a distinct product surface.

On narrow viewports reflow the five stat cards into a stacked or two-column wrapped layout, stack the two field groups vertically inside the converter panel, and keep the segmented control and primary CTA centered and legible.
