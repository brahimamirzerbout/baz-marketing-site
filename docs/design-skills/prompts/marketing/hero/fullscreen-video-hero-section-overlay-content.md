# Fullscreen Video Hero Section with Overlay Content

Create a full-viewport-height hero whose background is an autoplaying, looping, muted video that fills the entire section edge-to-edge. Layer a semi-transparent dimming gradient over the video so all foreground content remains readable.

**Top overlay** is a horizontal navigation bar absolutely positioned at the top of the section, sitting above both the video and the dimming layer.

**Bottom overlay** is a left-aligned or centered vertical stack anchored to the lower portion of the section, containing in order: a heading on its own line; a supporting paragraph directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width; a horizontal row of two CTA buttons sitting side by side on the same baseline (a primary button on the left and a secondary button on the right), placed directly below the paragraph. All overlay content sits above the video and above the dimming gradient layer.

On narrow viewports keep the same single-column stack for the bottom content, let the navigation bar compress or collapse into a mobile menu, and preserve reading order.
