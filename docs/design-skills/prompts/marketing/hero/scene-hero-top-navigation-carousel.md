# Scene Hero with Top Navigation and Carousel

Create a full-width hero whose background is one continuous photographic scene filling the section edge-to-edge.

**Top navigation bar** is pinned to the top of the section as a horizontal row composed of a brand word or mark on the left, a centered row of text nav links on the same baseline (some entries may include a small trailing chevron icon indicating a dropdown), and two compact action buttons on the right.

**Oversized type layer** sits in the upper-middle area of the frame: a single line of type scaled very large so it reads as a watermark or depth layer. Position it so it sits visually between the sky and the built subject, creating a sense of layered depth rather than flat body copy.

**Bottom-left anchor** holds a left-aligned vertical stack with comfortable inset padding, containing in order: a large headline on its own line; a short supporting sentence directly below the headline; one primary CTA button composed of a short label paired with a trailing arrow icon on its own line directly below the sentence.

**Bottom-right anchor** holds a compact carousel cluster composed of circular previous and next navigation controls on either side and a horizontal row of small rounded thumbnail previews between them on the same baseline (one preview carrying a clear selected state such as a highlighted border or scale treatment).

On narrow viewports keep the navigation bar usable (compress or collapse into a mobile menu), stack or reposition the bottom-left story block above the thumbnail strip, and preserve reading order throughout.
