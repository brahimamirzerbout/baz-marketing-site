# Centered Hero with Stats Row

Create a hero as one centered vertical stack on a full-width section background. Stack from top to bottom: a large centered heading on its own line; a centered supporting paragraph directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width; a horizontal row of two CTA buttons sitting side by side on the same baseline (a primary button on the left and a secondary button on the right), centered as a pair beneath the paragraph; finally, a horizontal row of three or four stat blocks on the same baseline directly below the button row, separated by thin vertical dividers. Each stat block is a compact vertical stack composed of one large prominent number on its own line at the top and a short descriptive label directly beneath the number. On narrow viewports keep the heading, paragraph, and buttons in a single-column centered stack, and collapse the stat row into a two-column grid with the dividers removed.
