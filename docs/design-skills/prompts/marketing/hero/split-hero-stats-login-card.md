# Split Hero with Stats and Login Card

Create a hero split into two columns on large 1280px width breakpoints on a full-width section surface that may include a very subtle geometric grid or dot texture in one area of the background. Horizontally center the two-column block within the section using a bounded max width so the pair does not stretch edge to edge on very wide viewports. On large breakpoints vertically center the left column as a single unit against the login card so the stacked content aligns with the vertical midpoint of the card.

**Left column** is a left-aligned vertical stack containing, in order: a prominent heading on its own line; a supporting paragraph directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width; one primary CTA button composed of a small leading video-style icon beside a short label on its own line directly below the paragraph. Below the button, place a horizontal row of three stat blocks on the same baseline; each block is a compact vertical stack composed of one large prominent numeric or short value on its own line at the top and a two-line descriptive label directly beneath the value.

**Right column** is a single rounded card with a light elevated treatment. Inside the card, stack from top to bottom: a compact header row composed of a small brand mark on the left and a short product name immediately to the right of the mark on the same baseline; a login title line on its own line directly below the header row; a secondary line of copy directly below the title containing a short sentence with an inline "sign up" text link at the end of the same line; a horizontal row of two secondary buttons of equal width sitting side by side for third-party sign-in (each button is composed of a small provider icon on the left and a short provider label to the right of the icon); a horizontal "or" separator made of a thin rule, a centered short word, and another thin rule; two labeled input rows stacked vertically (email and password) — each label sits above the input on its own line with a tiny required-field marker to the right of the label text; a single horizontal row composed of a small square "remember me" checkbox paired with an inline label on the left and a "forgot password" text link on the right; one full-width primary submit button at the bottom of the card.

On narrow viewports stack the two columns vertically with the left column first and the login card directly below it; keep all internal stacks in their original reading order.
