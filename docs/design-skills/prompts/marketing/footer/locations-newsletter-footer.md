# Locations Newsletter Footer

Create a footer section as one vertically stacked block inside a max-width container with comfortable side padding, composed of three distinct bands separated by generous vertical space.

**Locations band** — a horizontal row of three equal-width office blocks on large breakpoints with consistent gutters between them. Each block is a left-aligned vertical stack containing a bold city name on its own line at the top; a multi-line street address directly below the city name; a phone row composed of a small phone icon anchored to the left and a phone number to its right on the same baseline; and an email row composed of a small email icon anchored to the left and an email address to its right on the same baseline.

**Newsletter band** — a single horizontal row spanning the full container width containing a short label on the left (e.g. "Sign up to our newsletter") and an inline form group to its right on the same baseline, composed of a text input with a leading email icon and placeholder text paired with a compact primary submit button directly to its right.

**Bottom bar** — a horizontal row spanning the full container width containing, from left to right: a brand mark composed of a small logo icon paired with the brand name to its right on the same baseline; a centered copyright line; and a compact language selector composed of a small flag icon paired with a language label and a trailing chevron, anchored to the right end of the bar.

On narrow viewports stack the location blocks vertically in reading order; the newsletter label sits above the form input and button on its own line with the input and button stacking vertically beneath it; the bottom bar elements center and stack in order: brand mark, copyright, language selector.
