# Split 500 Error Page With Contact Support

Create a 500 error page as one centered vertical stack on a full-width section background, vertically centered within the viewport height. Stack from top to bottom: a small brand icon or logo mark centered horizontally on its own line; a brand name label centered on its own line directly below the icon; a large bold centered heading on its own line directly below the brand name stating the error status and a playful or empathetic descriptor (e.g. "Error, The internet gods are not happy"); a single centered paragraph of one or two sentences directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width, explaining that the site is experiencing issues and will return with improvements.

On narrow viewports keep the same single-column centered stack with comfortable side padding; allow the heading to wrap naturally while preserving center alignment throughout.
