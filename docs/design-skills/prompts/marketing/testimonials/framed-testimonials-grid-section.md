# Framed Testimonials Grid Section

Create a testimonials section split into two columns on large 1280px width breakpoints inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding.

The **left column** is a left-aligned vertical stack containing, in order: a small pill-shaped badge (composed of a short label inside a rounded container) on its own line; a large left-aligned multi-line testimonial quote paragraph directly below the badge at a comfortable reading width; and an author attribution row on its own line directly below the quote, composed of a small circular avatar thumbnail on the left, the author name immediately to the right of the avatar on the same baseline, a short separator character, and a role or company label to the right of the separator on the same baseline.

The **right column** contains a two-column by two-row grid of four partner blocks with consistent gutters between them. Each block is a centered vertical stack composed of, in order: a brand logo rendered at a comfortable display size centered at the top; a short partnership label on its own line directly below the logo (e.g. "Partner since 2015"); and a compact text link with a small leading icon and a short label (e.g. "See testimonial") centered on its own line directly below the partnership label.

On narrow viewports stack the two columns vertically with the testimonial quote column first and the partner grid second; the partner grid collapses into a single column on the narrowest breakpoints, preserving top-to-bottom reading order.
