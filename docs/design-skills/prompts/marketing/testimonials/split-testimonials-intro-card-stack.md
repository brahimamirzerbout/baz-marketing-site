# Split Testimonials Intro With Card Stack

Create a testimonials section split into two equal-width columns on large 1280px width breakpoints that spans the full available width of the page container. Place a thin vertical divider between the two columns.

The **left column** is a left-aligned vertical stack with comfortable internal padding containing, in order: a large left-aligned multi-line heading on its own line; a supporting paragraph directly beneath the heading at a comfortable reading width; and a social-proof row composed of a tight horizontal cluster of slightly overlapping small circular avatar thumbnails on the left and a single line of caption copy to their right that includes a numerically emphasized segment (e.g. a user count rendered in bold).

The **right column** uses a contrasting filled background surface that spans the full column area. Inside the column, place two testimonial cards stacked vertically with consistent spacing between them, vertically centered within the column. Each card is a rounded rectangular panel containing a left-aligned vertical stack composed of, in order: a bold testimonial title on its own line; a multi-line quote paragraph directly below the title; and an author attribution row at the bottom of the card composed of a small circular avatar thumbnail on the left, with a bold author name on its own line to the right of the avatar and a role or company label on a second line directly beneath the name, aligned with the name text.

On narrow viewports stack the two columns vertically with the intro column first and the testimonial-cards column second; each column stretches to the full content width and preserves all internal stacks in their original reading order.
