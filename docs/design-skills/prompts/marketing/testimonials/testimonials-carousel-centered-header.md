# Testimonials Carousel With Centered Header

Create a testimonials section inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding. At the top, place a centered header block composed of a small pill-shaped badge (containing a tiny leading icon and a short category label, both inside a rounded container) centered on its own line; a large centered heading on its own line directly below the badge; a centered supporting paragraph directly beneath the heading at a moderate reading width; and a horizontal row of two text links sitting side by side on the same baseline, centered as a pair beneath the paragraph — each link label includes a trailing arrow icon.

Below the header, place a horizontal carousel region spanning the full container width. The carousel displays three testimonial cards side by side in the visible frame with consistent gutters between them. Each card is a rounded rectangular panel containing a left-aligned vertical stack composed of, in order: a horizontal row of five small star icons on the same baseline at the top of the card; a bold testimonial title on its own line directly below the star row; a multi-line quote paragraph directly below the title; and an author attribution row at the bottom of the card composed of a small circular avatar thumbnail on the left, with a bold author name on its own line to the right of the avatar and a role or company label on a second line directly beneath the name, aligned with the name text. Position a circular previous-arrow navigation control on the left edge of the carousel and a circular next-arrow navigation control on the right edge, both vertically centered relative to the testimonial cards.

On narrow viewports the carousel shows one card at a time, the navigation controls remain anchored to the left and right edges, and the header links stack vertically if needed.
