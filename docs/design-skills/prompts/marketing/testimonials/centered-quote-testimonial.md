# Centered Quote Testimonial

Create a testimonials section inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding. Place a single centered vertical stack composed of, in order: a large decorative opening quotation mark glyph centered on its own line at the top; a centered multi-line quote paragraph directly below the quotation mark at a moderate reading width; and a centered author attribution row on its own line directly below the quote. The author row is a horizontal composition of a small circular avatar thumbnail on the left, the author name immediately to the right of the avatar on the same baseline, a short separator character, and a role or company label to the right of the separator on the same baseline.

On narrow viewports keep the same single-column centered stack; allow the quote paragraph to span the full content width with comfortable side padding.
