# Featured Testimonials Mosaic

Create a testimonials section inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding. At the top, place a centered header block composed of a large heading centered on its own line; a centered supporting paragraph directly beneath the heading at a moderate reading width; and a horizontal row of two text links sitting side by side on the same baseline, centered as a pair beneath the paragraph — each link label includes a trailing arrow icon.

Below the header, place a three-column layout on large 1280px width breakpoints. The **center column** is wider than the side columns and holds one tall featured testimonial card. The **left column** and the **right column** are narrower and equal in width; each stacks two smaller testimonial cards vertically with consistent spacing between them.

The **featured center card** is a tall rounded rectangular panel containing a centered vertical stack composed of, in order: a large decorative opening quotation mark glyph centered at the top; a centered multi-paragraph testimonial quote at a moderate reading width directly below the quotation mark; and a centered author attribution row at the bottom composed of a small circular avatar thumbnail on the left, with a bold author name on its own line to the right of the avatar and a role or company label on a second line directly beneath the name, aligned with the name text.

Each **small side card** is a compact rounded rectangular panel containing a left-aligned vertical stack composed of, in order: a bold testimonial title on its own line at the top; a short multi-line quote paragraph directly below the title; and an author attribution row at the bottom composed of a small circular avatar thumbnail on the left, with a bold author name on its own line to the right of the avatar and a role or company label on a second line directly beneath the name, aligned with the name text.

Below the three-column layout, centered directly beneath it on its own line, place a single compact button composed of a small leading icon and a short action label (e.g. "View more") on the same baseline.

On narrow viewports collapse the layout into a single column stacking the featured center card first, then the left-column cards, then the right-column cards in top-to-bottom reading order; each card stretches to the full content width with comfortable side padding.
