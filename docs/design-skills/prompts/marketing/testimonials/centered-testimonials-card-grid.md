# Centered Testimonials Card Grid

Create a testimonials section inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding. At the top, place a centered header block composed of a large heading centered on its own line; a centered supporting paragraph directly beneath the heading at a moderate reading width; and a horizontal row of two text links sitting side by side on the same baseline, centered as a pair beneath the paragraph — each link label includes a trailing arrow icon.

Below the header, place a three-column by three-row grid of nine testimonial cards with consistent gutters between columns and rows. Each card is a rounded rectangular panel containing a left-aligned vertical stack composed of, in order: a bold testimonial title on its own line at the top; a short multi-line quote paragraph directly below the title; and an author attribution row at the bottom of the card composed of a small circular avatar thumbnail on the left, with a bold author name on its own line to the right of the avatar and a role or company label on a second line directly beneath the name, aligned with the name text. Allow a very subtle geometric pattern (such as a honeycomb or hexagonal motif) behind the grid area so it reads as a soft decorative surface without competing with the card content.

Below the grid, centered directly beneath it on its own line, place a single compact button composed of a small leading icon and a short action label (e.g. "View more") on the same baseline.

On narrow viewports reduce the column count stepwise (three columns, then two, then one) until the cards form a single column on the narrowest breakpoints; preserve left-to-right top-to-bottom reading order throughout.
