# Centered Portfolio Image Card Grid

Create a portfolio section inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding. At the top, place a centered heading on its own line; directly below the heading, a centered supporting paragraph at a comfortable reading width.

Below the header block, place a three-column by two-row grid of six equal-width cards with consistent gutters between columns and rows. Inside each card, stack from top to bottom: a large image with rounded corners spanning the full card width at the top; a small pill-shaped category badge directly below the image composed of a tiny leading icon and a short label inside a rounded container; a project title on its own line directly below the badge; a short description paragraph directly below the title; and a horizontal row of two buttons at the bottom of the card sitting side by side on the same baseline — a primary button on the left composed of a short label paired with a trailing arrow icon, and a secondary outlined button on the right composed of a small leading external-link icon and a short label.

On narrow viewports, collapse the grid stepwise from three columns to two, then to a single column on the narrowest breakpoints; preserve left-to-right top-to-bottom reading order throughout.
