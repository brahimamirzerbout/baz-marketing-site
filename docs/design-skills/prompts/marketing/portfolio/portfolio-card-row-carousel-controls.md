# Portfolio Card Row With Carousel Controls

Create a portfolio section inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding. At the top, place a left-aligned vertical stack containing, in order: a large multi-line heading on its own line; a supporting paragraph directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width; two stacked text links below the paragraph, each on its own line and composed of a short label paired with a trailing arrow icon.

Below the header block, place a horizontal row of three equal-width cards on large 1280px width breakpoints with consistent gutters between them. Inside each card, stack from top to bottom: a brand logo or mark left-aligned on its own line; a project title on its own line directly below the logo; a text link on its own line directly below the title composed of a short label paired with a small trailing external-link icon; a short description paragraph directly below the link; and a compact outlined button at the bottom of the card composed of a small leading icon and a short label.

Centered directly below the card row on its own line, place a horizontal pair of circular navigation controls (a left-pointing arrow and a right-pointing arrow) for paging through additional cards.

On narrow viewports, show one card at a time with the navigation arrows remaining visible below the active card; allow horizontal swiping or arrow taps to advance through the set.
