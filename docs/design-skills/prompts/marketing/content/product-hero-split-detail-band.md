# Product Hero With Split Detail Band

Create a content section as a vertical stack inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding.

The **header row** sits at the top. Place a large left-aligned product or brand heading on its own line; on the same row or directly beneath the heading, place two text-style links in a horizontal row (one link may pair with a small leading external-link icon and the other with a small trailing arrow icon). Keep consistent spacing between the heading and the link row.

The **hero image** sits below the header row. Span one wide horizontal image that fills the full content width; the image carries rounded corners and a placeholder or photographic background.

The **body band** sits below the hero image as a two-column split on large 1280px width breakpoints. The two columns share the same top alignment.

The **left column** of the body band is a left-aligned vertical stack containing, in order: an introductory paragraph on its own; then a vertical list of eight feature lines with consistent spacing between them — each line is a horizontal row pairing a small leading icon at the left with a single-line label to the right of the icon on the same baseline.

The **right column** of the body band is a left-aligned vertical stack containing three multi-line paragraphs arranged one after another with consistent vertical spacing between them.

On narrow viewports, stack the left column above the right column; the hero image remains full width and the feature list and prose paragraphs occupy the single column in their original reading order.
