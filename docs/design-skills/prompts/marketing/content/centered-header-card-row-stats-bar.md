# Centered Header With Card Row and Stats Bar

Create a content section as a vertical stack inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding.

The **header block** sits at the top and is horizontally centered: first a small pill-shaped badge combining a tiny leading icon at the left with a short label to its right (the badge sits on its own centered line); then a large centered heading on its own line directly beneath the badge; then a single centered text-style link whose label includes a small trailing arrow icon directly beneath the heading.

The **card row** sits below the header block. Arrange three equal-width cards in one horizontal row with consistent gutters between them. Each card is a vertical stack containing, in order: a circular icon or illustration region at the top (centered horizontally within the card); a medium heading on its own line directly beneath the icon; a short description paragraph beneath the heading at the card's reading width; and a compact secondary button whose label pairs with a small leading book icon at the left end, anchored at the bottom of the card stack.

The **statistics bar** sits below the card row as a full-width band spanning the entire content area. Place four horizontal stat items in one row with consistent spacing. Each stat item is a compact inline group showing one large numeric or shorthand value and a short descriptive label beside or directly beneath it.

On narrow viewports, stack the three cards vertically with consistent spacing between them; reflow the statistics bar into a two-by-two grid or a vertical stack as needed.
