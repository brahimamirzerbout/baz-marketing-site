# Editorial Hero With Split Article Content

Create a content section as a vertical stack inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding.

The **headline band** sits at the top. Place a large heading on its own line; a supporting paragraph sits directly beneath the heading (the paragraph may span one or two columns depending on available width and is left-aligned with the heading).

The **hero image** sits below the headline band. Span one full-width horizontal image that fills the entire content area; the image carries rounded corners and a placeholder or photographic background.

The **article band** sits below the hero image as a two-column layout on large 1280px width breakpoints. The two columns share the same top alignment.

The **left column** of the article band is a left-aligned vertical stack containing two paragraphs arranged one after another with consistent vertical spacing between them.

The **right column** of the article band is a left-aligned vertical stack containing three paragraphs arranged one after another with consistent vertical spacing between them.

On narrow viewports, stack everything in a single column in reading order: heading, paragraph, hero image, then all article paragraphs merged into one continuous column (left-column paragraphs first, right-column paragraphs second) preserving their original sequence.
