# Filterable Team Photo Grid

Create a team section inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding. At the top, place a centered header stack composed of a small pill-shaped badge on its own line (containing a tiny leading icon and a short label); a large centered multi-line heading directly below the badge; and a centered supporting paragraph directly beneath the heading at a moderate reading width.

Below the header, place a single horizontal row of category filter controls centered on the page (e.g. "Board members", "Developers", "Designers", "Marketing", "Support"). Each control is composed of a small leading icon and a short label on the same baseline; one control carries a distinct active treatment while the remaining controls use a plain text appearance.

Below the filter row, place a responsive grid of team member photo blocks arranged as four columns and two rows on the widest breakpoint, with consistent gutters between all blocks. Each block is a large rounded rectangular container filled edge-to-edge by a portrait photograph. At the bottom of each container, overlay the team member's name on its own line and a single-line job title directly below the name — both positioned over the lower portion of the image.

Below the grid, place a centered footer bar on its own line composed of a small leading icon, a short bold prompt (e.g. "Want to join?"), a supporting sentence to its right on the same baseline, and a text-style link with a trailing arrow icon anchored to the right end of the bar.

On smaller breakpoints reduce the column count stepwise (four, then two, then one); the filter row scrolls horizontally or wraps; preserve left-to-right top-to-bottom reading order throughout.
