# Team Testimonial With Photo Grid

Create a team section split into two columns on large 1280px width breakpoints inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding.

At the top of the section, place a left-aligned header block composed of a large heading on its own line followed by a supporting paragraph directly beneath the heading at a comfortable reading width. Below the paragraph, place a single text-style link on its own line whose label includes a trailing arrow icon.

Below the header, the **left column** contains a single tall testimonial card with rounded corners and a thin outer border. Inside the card, stack from top to bottom: a large decorative quotation mark centered horizontally; a multi-line quote paragraph centered within the card; and an attribution row composed of a small circular avatar on the left, the testimonial author's name to the right of the avatar on the same baseline, and a secondary line of the author's role or handle directly beneath the name.

The **right column** contains a two-column by two-row grid of four team member photo blocks with consistent gutters between them. Each block is a rounded rectangular container filled edge-to-edge by a large portrait photograph. At the bottom of each photo container, overlay a compact content strip containing the team member's name on its own line and a single-line job title directly below the name — the strip sits above the image with a short accent bar or indicator along one edge to visually frame the text.

On narrow viewports stack the two columns vertically with the testimonial card first and the photo grid directly beneath it; the four photo blocks reflow into a two-column arrangement or a single column on the narrowest breakpoints.
