# Two-Column Team Intro With Contacts

Create a team section split into two columns on large 1280px width breakpoints inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding.

The **left column** is a left-aligned vertical stack containing, in order: a small pill-shaped badge on its own line (composed of a short category label inside a rounded container); a large left-aligned multi-line heading on its own line directly below the badge; a supporting paragraph directly beneath the heading at a comfortable reading width; two text-style links stacked vertically, each on its own line, whose labels include a trailing arrow icon.

Allow a very subtle geometric grid texture to appear behind the junction between the two columns as a soft decorative surface that does not compete with the content.

The **right column** contains a horizontal row of two team member blocks sitting side by side with consistent spacing between them. Each block is a centered vertical stack containing, in order: a medium circular avatar image at the top; the team member's name on its own line directly below the avatar in medium weight; a single-line job title directly below the name in lighter weight; a short contact-detail row composed of a small leading icon and an email address on the same baseline; and a second contact-detail row composed of a small leading icon and a phone number on the same baseline directly below the email row.

On narrow viewports stack the two columns vertically with the intro column first and the team member blocks directly beneath it; the two member blocks stack vertically in reading order.
