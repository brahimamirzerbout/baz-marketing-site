# Centered FAQ Accordion Section

Create a FAQ section inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding. At the top, place a large centered heading on its own line.

Below the heading, place a vertical stack of 6-8 accordion items with consistent spacing between them, constrained to a moderate max width and centered horizontally within the container. Each item is a self-contained collapsible row: in its collapsed state, the row is a horizontal composition of a small question-mark icon anchored to the left, a bold question label to the right of the icon on the same baseline, and a small chevron icon anchored to the far right of the row pointing downward. When expanded, the chevron rotates upward and a content region appears directly below the question row, containing one or more paragraphs of answer text at a comfortable reading width; inline text links may appear within the answer copy. Only one item should be expandable at a time (opening one collapses any previously open item).

On narrow viewports keep the single-column accordion stack centered; preserve reading order and ensure question rows and their answer regions remain full-width within the container.
