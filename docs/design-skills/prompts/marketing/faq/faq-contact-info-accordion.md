# FAQ Contact Info With Accordion

Create a FAQ section split into two columns on large breakpoints inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding.

The **left column** is a left-aligned vertical stack containing, in order: a large left-aligned heading on its own line; a supporting paragraph directly beneath the heading at a comfortable reading width; a contact information block composed of two inline groups on the same baseline — the first group pairs a small location icon on the left with a multi-line address to its right, the second group pairs a small information icon on the left with a contact label and a linked email address to its right; and a compact button on its own line below the contact block whose label is preceded by a small leading icon.

The **right column** contains a vertical stack of accordion items with consistent spacing between them. Each item follows the same collapsible pattern: a horizontal row composed of a small question-mark icon on the left, a bold question label to the right of the icon on the same baseline, and a small chevron icon anchored to the far right of the row. When expanded, the chevron rotates and a content region appears directly below the question row containing one or more paragraphs of answer text at a comfortable reading width; inline text links may appear within the answer copy.

On narrow viewports stack the two columns vertically with the intro-and-contact column first and the accordion column directly below it; keep all internal stacks in their original reading order.
