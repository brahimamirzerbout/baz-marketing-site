# Split Newsletter Signup With Image

Create a newsletter section split into two columns on large 1280px width breakpoints inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding.

**Left column** is a left-aligned vertical stack containing, in order: a small pill-shaped badge composed of a short label on its own line; a large left-aligned heading on its own line directly below the badge; a supporting paragraph directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width; a full-width email text input on its own line placed below the paragraph after extra vertical space; a full-width primary subscribe button directly below the input (the button label includes a trailing arrow icon); and a horizontal row of short category labels sitting side by side on the same baseline directly below the button, each label composed of a small checkmark icon on the left and a short text to its right.

**Right column** holds one large image with rounded corners that fills the column area, vertically centered relative to the left column content.

On narrow viewports stack the two columns vertically with the content column first and the image directly below it; keep all internal stacks in their original reading order.
