# Framed Newsletter Signup Card

Create a newsletter section inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding, framed as one wide rounded container that reads as a single card spanning the full container width. Allow a very subtle geometric grid or line texture to appear in one area of the card background as a soft decorative accent without competing with the content.

Inside the card, split into two columns on large 1280px width breakpoints with comfortable internal padding on all sides.

**Left column** is a left-aligned vertical stack containing, in order: a left-aligned heading on its own line; a supporting paragraph directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width; and a social-proof row placed below the paragraph after extra vertical space, composed of a tight horizontal cluster of slightly overlapping small circular user avatars on the left and a single line of caption copy to their right that includes a numerically emphasized segment (e.g. a subscriber or reader count rendered in bold) — both sitting on the same baseline.

**Right column** is vertically centered relative to the left column and contains a compact form stack, in order: a horizontal row on the same baseline composed of a full-width email text input with a small leading mail icon on the left and a primary subscribe button anchored to the right of the input; and a short line of privacy copy directly below the form row, containing an inline text link (e.g. "Privacy Policy").

On narrow viewports stack the two columns vertically with the copy column first and the form column directly below it; keep all internal stacks in their original reading order and the card container at full content width with comfortable side padding.
