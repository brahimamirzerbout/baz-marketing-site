# Newsletter Benefits With Signup Form

Create a newsletter section split into two columns on large 1280px width breakpoints inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding.

**Left column** contains a vertical stack of three benefit blocks with consistent spacing between them. Each block is a horizontal row composed of a small rounded icon container on the left (holding a single centered glyph) and a text block to the right of the icon containing a bold title on its own line and a short description paragraph directly beneath the title. Each block may carry a subtle outer boundary or light background so it reads as a distinct item.

**Right column** is a left-aligned vertical stack containing, in order: a left-aligned heading on its own line; a supporting paragraph directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width; a short form label on its own line below the paragraph after extra vertical space, paired with a small required-field marker and an optional hint icon to its right; a full-width email text input with a small leading mail icon directly below the label; a full-width primary subscribe button directly below the input; and a short line of privacy copy directly below the button, containing an inline text link (e.g. "Privacy Policy").

On narrow viewports stack the two columns vertically with the benefit list first and the form column directly below it; keep all internal stacks in their original reading order.
