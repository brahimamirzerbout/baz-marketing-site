# Framed Portrait Newsletter Signup

Create a newsletter section inside a centered page container with comfortable side padding. Position four large circular portrait photographs as decorative anchors around the perimeter of the section — one near the top-left, one near the top-right, one near the bottom-left, and one near the bottom-right — so they frame the central content without overlapping it.

In the center of the section, place a vertical stack containing, in order: a large centered heading on its own line; a centered supporting paragraph directly below the heading at a comfortable reading width; a horizontal row on the same baseline composed of a full-width email text input on the left and a primary subscribe button on the right (the button label includes a trailing arrow icon), centered as a pair beneath the paragraph and constrained to a moderate width. Directly below the subscribe row, place a centered social-proof line composed of a tight horizontal cluster of slightly overlapping small circular user avatars on the left, a small "+" separator, and a short caption stating a subscriber count — all sitting on the same baseline.

On narrow viewports keep the same single-column centered stack; let the email input and subscribe button wrap into a vertical pair if needed; keep the social-proof row centered below.
