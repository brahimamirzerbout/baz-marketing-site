# Customer Case Study Card Grid

Create a customer logos section that presents customers as individual cards rather than a bare logo strip, arranged inside a page-width container with comfortable side padding. Use a responsive grid of equal-width cards on large 1280px width breakpoints (for example three or four cards per row).

Inside **each card**, stack from top to bottom: the customer mark centered horizontally at the top of the card; a one-line company name or story title on its own line directly beneath the mark; a single short supporting sentence below the title providing context for the case study; and at the bottom of the card a text link whose label includes a trailing arrow character, pointing to the full case study. Limit each card to one primary mark so the section stays scannable and visually uniform.

On narrow viewports the cards reflow into a single column, each card occupying the full content width with comfortable side padding; keep all internal stacks in their original reading order.
