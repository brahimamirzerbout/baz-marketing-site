# Centered Logo Card Grid

Create a customer logos section as one centered vertical stack inside a page-width container with comfortable side padding.

The **header block** sits at the top of the stack, centered horizontally, and contains in order from top to bottom: a primary heading on its own line; one supporting paragraph directly beneath the heading, held at a restrained max width so the line length stays comfortable; and below the paragraph a single horizontal row of two text links sitting side by side with a small gap between them (each link label includes a trailing arrow character at the end of the wording).

Leave clear vertical space beneath the header block, then present six partner marks, each placed inside its own card with the mark centered both horizontally and vertically within the card. Arrange the six cards in two centered rows of three cards each: the first row holds three cards, the second row holds three cards directly below, with equal horizontal gaps between cards and equal vertical gaps between the two rows.

On narrow viewports reflow the cards into fewer columns (for example two columns or a single column) while keeping the header block centered above and allowing the link row to wrap onto a second line if space is tight.
