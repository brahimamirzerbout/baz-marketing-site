# Centered Customer Logo Band

Create a customer logos section as one centered vertical stack inside a page-width container with comfortable side padding. At the very top, place a short centered kicker line on its own row naming the social-proof intent (for example a sentence stating that the product is trusted by teams worldwide). Below the kicker, leave a small vertical gap, then center a single horizontal flex row of exactly six partner marks or logotypes that share one visual weight; space the marks with even horizontal gaps so they read as one unified band. No secondary heading is required unless you add a very small optional sub-line directly beneath the kicker on its own centered row. On narrow viewports the mark row wraps into additional centered rows while keeping even horizontal spacing between items and consistent vertical gaps between wrapped lines.
